Course Material

This class is a rapid learning course on computer programming and programmatic thinking. The subject is aimed at students with little or no programming experience and is designed to provide a solid foundation for future independent study.

History

Computer science is as much about computers as astronomy is about telescopes. We'll look at how the very concept of computation came to be and the major figures who established it.

Philosophy

Asking whether a computer can think is like asking whether a submarine can swim. The answer is "Yes, but faster!" Students will learn about diverse foundational programming issues.

JavaScript

Course materials will cover control structures, data types, and functional programming. Students will come away from this class with their own original code that they’ll be able to build off of.

Career Hacking

I'll share secrets I've learned about how to become a successful developer. This includes what to focus on in your studies and how to present your self to potential customers and employers.
